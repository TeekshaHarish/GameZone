How to Play Snake Game

1. Objective: Control the snake, help it eat food, and avoid collisions.
2. Controls: Use arrow keys or WASD to control the snake's movement.
3. Gameplay: The snake moves continuously in the current direction. Eat food to grow longer and increase your score. Avoid colliding with the snake's body and game boundaries.
4. Scoring and Game Over: Each time the snake eats food, your score increases. The game ends if the snake collides with itself or the boundaries. Your final score will be displayed.
5. Game Over and Reset: The game ends if the snake collides with itself or the boundaries.Restart the game by pressing the Reset button or refreshing the page.
Enjoy playing the Snake Game and have fun!
